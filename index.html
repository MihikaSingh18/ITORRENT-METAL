About Our Founder

With over 15 years of experience in the metal industry, Mr. Shubham Bishnoi founded itorrent Metal with a vision to provide premium quality metal solutions to industries across India. His expertise in metallurgy and commitment to excellence has made itorrent Metal a trusted name in the industry.

Starting his career as a metallurgical engineer, Shubham witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by industries in sourcing high-quality metal products. This inspired him to establish itorrent Metal, focusing on precision cutting, custom fabrication, and timely delivery.

Under his leadership, the company has grown from a small operation to serving major industrial clients across Maharashtra and beyond, maintaining the highest standards of quality and customer satisfaction.
